# Workshop Pro — Privacy Policy

**Version 1.0 — Effective date: May 2026**

## Overview

Workshop Pro is offline-first desktop software. By default, customer, vehicle, staff, and billing data you enter is stored **only on your computer** in a local SQLite database.

## Data we collect (in the application)

You may store:

- Customer names, phone numbers, addresses
- Vehicle registration and service history
- Staff names, roles, and attendance
- Invoices, payments, and inventory records
- Workshop profile and GST details

This data is under your control. The publisher does not receive it unless you explicitly use a future cloud or support feature that transmits data.

## Data location

| Item | Typical location |
|------|------------------|
| Database | `%APPDATA%/workshop-pro/data/workshop.db` (Windows) or `~/Library/Application Support/workshop-pro/` (macOS) |
| Backups | `workshop-pro/backups/` under application data |
| PDF exports | User Documents folder |

## Your responsibilities

- Restrict physical and OS-level access to workshop computers
- Use strong admin and staff PINs
- Run regular backups to external storage
- Comply with local laws when handling customer personal data

## Updates and telemetry

Production builds may check for application updates (version metadata only). No customer business data is sent with update checks unless explicitly configured otherwise in a future release.

## Third-party components

Workshop Pro includes open-source libraries (Electron, React, Prisma, etc.). Their licenses are listed in `NOTICE.md`.
